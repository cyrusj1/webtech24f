Laws and regulations for Commercial and Headboats

  • Size Limits For Massachusetts
    1. Commercial can keep to sell anything over 73 inches
    2. Headboat is allowed to keep above or below 73 inches
  • How many can you keep
    1. The law changes frequently based on time of year but is ussualy one
  • When can I fish for them
    1. There is no set season In MA for tuna allowing you to fish anytime of year but the are restricted fishing days for commercial allowing them to only fish 3 times a week.
