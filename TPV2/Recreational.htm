Laws and regulations for recreational fisherman

  • Size Limits For Massachusetts
    1. Recreational can keep a tuna over 23Inches
    2. Yet the Tuna has to be less that 37 Inches
  • How many can you keep
    1. Recreational fisherman can keep 1 per boat per day and they have to reported at end of day
  • When can I fish for them
    1. There is no set season In MA for tuna allowing you to fish anytime of year. But they arent always catchable at all times of the year.
