Early Life

Bluefin Tuna spawn once a year, typically in the gulf Mexico. This has Tuna listed as a highly migratory species because they undertake the long journey to spawn. Tuna produce millions of eggs each year which are fertlized in the water. After fertilization they drift in the water for months. Bluefin Tuna reach maturity at about 5 years old or roughly 5 feet and 130LBS.

Life as the big fish in the sea

Tuna eat a wide variety of food including squid, baitfish, and crustaceans. They eat up to 5% of there body weight each day. However there diet is Completly dependednt of there location and size. Bluefin tuna can get massive as a 600lbs fish caught off of Boston is impressive not cause of the size but because of the dedication needed. The largest Bluefin ever caught was 1496lbs off of Nova Scotia, Canada. Most mature adult Blue Fin tuna are 6-8 feet long and weigh 400-600lbs.
