Hello I am Cyrus,

I lived inbetween Boston and CapeCod for the majority if my life. When I was in 7th grade I biked 2 miles to the local abit shop in my town and asked for a job. I then worked there till my freshman year of college. At the bait shop I learned how to fish at an elite level. I also got to learn about the #1 game fish just a few miles from the shore of Massachusetts.

At this bait shop I was also given the opportunity to work on BlueFin tuna boats as a mate. Where my job was often setting up gear, keeping customers happy, and most of making sure the trip ran smoothly. I also was given the opportunity to learn how to build custome fishing poles which was a great way for me to make money doing what I love durring the winter.
