Harpoon Tuna Fishing

Harpooning for Tuna also called stick boat is a commercial only way to catch them. Less common than rod a reel Harpoon in the capecod area. Harpoon boats often use a plane in the sky to get cordinated on where the tuna are swimming close to the surface. When they have these cordinate they storm over and throw a harpoon from the front of the boat. After they hit the fish a strong eletric current is sent through a wire into the harpoon killing the fish instantly. This helps keep the meat tasting better as it doesnt overheat in a fight. The downside to harpoon boats as the water has to be extremely calm to be able to see a fish from a far distance.
